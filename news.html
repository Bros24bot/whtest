30 July – 3 August 2025

One Team – Five Alliances – 30 July

On 30 July 2025, a major milestone was reached in State 214’s history. Through outstanding teamwork and unity, SRC, DOA, PBT, ODA, and BKK joined forces and registered together for the legendary Frostdragon Tyrant event. Over 180 players participated – made possible by shared planning and unified leadership.

From these five alliances, three combat groups were formed to coordinate the attack. This alliance-wide strategy transformed a challenging event into a structured team effort.

The Battle Begins – 3 August

On 3 August, State 214’s united forces faced the mighty Frostdragon Tyrant. The coordination between alliances paid off: despite fierce competition, we proudly secured a strong second place, narrowly missing the top spot.

The result reflected excellent preparation, trust across alliances, and the shared will to act as one State. This teamwork made the difference.

Teamwork in Action

Five alliances, one vision – and three unified fighting groups. This event marked more than just participation: it showed that with planning and cooperation, State 214 can achieve great things through collective strength.

Teamwork. Unity. One Goal – State 214.
