30 July – 16 August 2025

One Team – Five Alliances – 30 July

On 30 July 2025, a major milestone was reached in State 214’s history. Through outstanding teamwork and unity, SRC, DOA, PBT, ODA, and BKK joined forces and registered together for the legendary Frostdragon Tyrant event. Over 180 players participated – made possible by shared planning and unified leadership.

From these five alliances, three combat groups were formed to coordinate the attack. This alliance-wide strategy transformed a challenging event into a structured team effort.

The Battle Begins – 3 August

On 3 August, State 214’s united forces faced the mighty Frostdragon Tyrant. The coordination between alliances paid off: despite fierce competition, we proudly secured a strong second place, narrowly missing the top spot.

The result reflected excellent preparation, trust across alliances, and the shared will to act as one State. This teamwork made the difference.

Teamwork in Action

Five alliances, one vision – and three unified fighting groups. This event marked more than just participation: it showed that with planning and cooperation, State 214 can achieve great things through collective strength.

Teamwork. Unity. One Goal – State 214.

SVS Victory – 16 August

On 16 August 2025, State 214 achieved a historic triumph in the State vs State competition against State 249. Already in the preparation phase, our State secured a clear lead through excellent organization and coordination.

During the battle phase, our united forces conquered the enemy’s castle of State 249 – a spectacular success and proof of courage, strategy, and teamwork.

This victory was only possible thanks to the joint effort of our alliances: ODA, PBT, BKK, SRC, and DOA. Together we have shown that State 214 stands strong as one.

Congratulations to all fighters of State 214 – this success belongs to us all!

Another Triumph – 13 September

On 13 September 2025, State 214 once again proved its strength in the State vs State competition – this time against the powerful State 194. Through determination and unity, we achieved a clear victory.

Already in the early hours of the preparation phase, our coordination and discipline created a decisive lead. When the battle phase began, our players attacked with precision, defending our territory while striking the enemy with force.

The clash with State 194 highlighted what makes our community special: strategy, courage, and teamwork across all alliances. Each fighter contributed to the result – no matter if in rallies, defenses, or support.

With this victory, State 214 continues its path of success, showing that our vision and unity carry us forward.

Congratulations to every player – together we made history once again!
