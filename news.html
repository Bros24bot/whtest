Historic Alliance Coordination – 30 July 2025

Posted on 30 July 2025

In an impressive display of teamwork, alliances from State 214 came together to register for the Frostdragon Tyrant event. Through the joint effort of SRC, DOA, PBT, ODA, and BKK, over 180 players were organized for participation — a powerful example of unity and preparation. The coordination between these alliances laid the foundation for one of the most synchronized entries in the event's history.

Frostdragon Tyrant Battle – 3 August 2025

Posted on 3 August 2025

During the Frostdragon Tyrant event, State 214 delivered a strong performance, securing a well-earned second place. While the top spot was just out of reach, our alliances proved that collaboration and strategy can lead to great achievements. Minor setbacks were swiftly overcome, and the result highlighted the trust and respect that binds our community together.

What's Next?

Ongoing

This milestone is just the beginning. With continued cooperation and mutual support, State 214 is well-positioned for future success in upcoming events. The community remains committed to fair play, inclusion, and strengthening bonds between players across all alliances.
