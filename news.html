30 July – 3 August 2025

One Team – Five Alliances – 30 July

On 30 July 2025, a major milestone was reached in State 214’s history. Through outstanding teamwork and unity, SRC, DOA, PBT, ODA, and BKK jointly registered for the legendary Frostdragon Tyrant event. Over 180 players took part – a result only made possible through seamless collaboration.

It was not just a tactical move – it was a sign of strategic unity. Trust, planning, and cooperation created something unique: a State team effort beyond individual alliances.

The Battle Begins – 3 August

On 3 August, State 214’s alliances went into battle against the mighty Frostdragon Tyrant. Thanks to focused preparation and coordination, we secured a strong second place, only narrowly missing the top spot.

This performance highlights our state’s dedication and collective strength. While challenges arose, they were overcome – proving the potential of united action in Whiteout Survival.

Teamwork in Action

Five alliances, one vision: to represent State 214 as a single, powerful unit. This event marked a turning point in collaboration – not just a fight, but a statement of unity.

Teamwork. Unity. One Goal – State 214.
