📢 Please respect all guidelines during the event

1️⃣ Kill Event – Brothers in Arms (BIA)

1️⃣ Attacking Resource Points

❌ Not allowed. Attacking resource gathering points is strictly prohibited.

2️⃣ Attacking NAP 5, SUB Members, or Players Inside NAP 5/SUB Base or HQ

❌ Not allowed. Do not attack players under NAP 5, SUB alliances, or those located in NAP 5/SUB base or HQ.

3️⃣ Attacking Players Outside of NAP or Without an Alliance

✅ Allowed. You may attack these players without any restrictions.

4️⃣ Castle Rotation

✅ The top 5 alliances will rotate control of the castle every 2 weeks. This ensures that every alliance within the Big 5 has an opportunity to control the castle.

5️⃣ Facility Assignment

✅ Facilities within the state will be shared and assigned on a fixed basis. Each alliance will be assigned its designated facilities to ensure efficient operations during the event.
