📢 KE Rules – Please respect all guidelines during the event

1️⃣ Attacking Resource Points

❌ Not allowed. Attacking resource gathering points is strictly prohibited.

2️⃣ Attacking NAP 5, SUB Members, or Players Inside NAP 5/SUB Base or HQ

❌ Not allowed. Do not attack players under NAP 5, SUB alliances, or those located in NAP 5/SUB base or HQ.

3️⃣ Attacking Players Outside of NAP or Without an Alliance

✅ Allowed. You may attack these players without any restrictions.
