Growth

Through countless battles, strategic decisions, and community efforts, State 214 has become one of the most successful and respected states in the game. Its strength lies in the cohesion and teamwork fostered among its players, allowing for consistent growth and achievement.

Losses and Challenges: January - March 2024

From January to March 2024, State 214 experienced significant losses. We saw the dissolution of several key alliances, including AXE (formerly TEX), BLK, and part of USA, as they began to disband and leave the state. This period was a challenging time, as our best alliances and dear friends, such as TDS/TLS, also followed suit, leaving a void.

Challenges with New Alliances: September - December 2024

In September 2024, we welcomed a new alliance that initially joined State 214 with good intentions. However, after a short period, some members of this alliance showed a lack of interest in collaboration and instead spread chaos and discord. As a unified state, we faced this challenge together, particularly with the support of ODA. In December 2024, the entire state took a stand against the disruptive players. By January 2025, after much unrest, the remaining members of this alliance were forced to consider and eventually execute a transfer, thus restoring order and unity within State 214.
