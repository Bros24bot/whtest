From Foundation to Frost: The Rise of State 214

State 214 officially launched in April 2023, stepping into the world of Whiteout Survival with fierce competition and dynamic leadership.

Like every great state, 214 faced its early challenges — power struggles, shifting alliances, and growing pains. But unlike many others, we quickly stabilized through diplomacy, teamwork, and the shared belief that community matters more than control.

Through countless battles and events, 214 evolved into one of the most successful and respected states in the game. With strong coordination and alliance synergy, we’ve continued to grow together.

The Merge with State 196 – Winter 2025

During the great state fusion in winter 2025, we welcomed amazing players from State 196. This merge was a breakthrough — boosting power, diversity, and ambition.

The community gained not only numbers, but passion. Together, we restructured and refined 214 into a modern, balanced and competitive powerhouse.

Recent Progress & Future Goals

Just last month, a major shift happened: PBT made a successful alliance transition, joined by numerous skilled players during the most recent transfer window. These changes further pushed us forward.

Today, our sights are set on the Frost Tyrant — not only deploying 3 top alliances, but claiming the Frost Tyrant itself in the name of State 214.
